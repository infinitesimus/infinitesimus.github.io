Virtual Reality

Virtual reality is the next big medium. Some even say it's the final medium. Whatever it is, VR will change the world.

I want to be part of that.

Having traveled the world, I've seen extraodinary beauty, felt empowering freedom, and experienced daring adventures. These are unique things I want to bring to virtual reality, and I think they're important; The first virtual worlds will be inspired from our current reality with improvements. I can spring those virtual worlds alive by drawing from my traveling experiences. And I can materialize those ideas with my background in engineering, design, and entrepreneurship.

To achieve this, I'm looking to find the most talented and passionate VR developers and designers in Vancouver.

I have nearly a decade of experience in starting and running businesses, having worked in all facets of business from negotiating licensing terms to marketing to programming. So, I can speak your language without getting in the way. I'm also experienced in Unity and can script in C# to design intuitive and purpose-driven user experiences in VR.

Contact me if you want to build a metaverse together.

About Me

When I was young, I remember sitting in a brightly-lit room attending art class. Beside me, there was a large canvas hanging on the wall that laid out a Fulfillment Wheel. It depicted a circle partitioned into seven partsâ€”each part representing seven key areas of personal fulfillment: intellectual, career, social, physical, family, financial, and spiritual.

I didn't pay too much attention to it then, only gazing at it when my mind wandered off in class. My only thought was that it would be great to have all parts in equal proportions. This seemingly tacky wheel has continued to make an impact on me to this very day.

It has lead me to value balance and diversity.

It made me want to become a polymath.
